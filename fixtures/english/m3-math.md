# Math Fidelity Demo

Folio renders LaTeX math as native, editable Word equations — not images.

## Inline math

The energy of a photon is $E = h\nu$ where $h$ is Planck's constant.
For a free particle, $p = mv$ and the kinetic energy is $\frac{1}{2}mv^2$.

## Display math

Einstein's mass–energy equivalence:

$$E = mc^2$$

The quadratic formula:

$$x = \frac{-b \pm \sqrt{b^2 - 4ac}}{2a}$$

A matrix:

$$\begin{pmatrix} a & b \\ c & d \end{pmatrix}$$

End of demo.
